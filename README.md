# NemoChat Support

Support page for **NemoChat** — an offline iOS AI assistant powered by on-device inference. No cloud, no tracking, your conversations stay on your device.

## Getting Help

If you're experiencing an issue or have a question about NemoChat:

1. **Browse existing issues** to see if your question has already been answered: [Issues](https://github.com/theabecaster/chatnemo-support/issues)
2. **Open a new issue** if you don't find an answer: [New Issue](https://github.com/theabecaster/chatnemo-support/issues/new)

When reporting a bug, please include:

- **Device model** (e.g., iPhone 15 Pro)
- **iOS version** (e.g., iOS 17.4)
- **NemoChat version** (shown in Settings)
- **Steps to reproduce** the issue
- **Expected vs. actual behavior**

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Does NemoChat send my data to a server?
No. All inference runs on-device. Your conversations never leave your iPhone.

### Which devices are supported?
NemoChat requires iPhone 15 Pro or newer running iOS 17+. On-device inference needs the memory and Neural Engine capabilities of these devices.

### Why is the model download so large?
The AI model (Gemma) is bundled as a quantized GGUF file. The download is a one-time cost — after that, the model runs entirely offline.

### Can I use NemoChat without an internet connection?
Yes. After the initial model download, NemoChat works fully offline.

## Privacy

NemoChat does not collect, transmit, or store your conversations on any external server. See the in-app Privacy section for full details.
